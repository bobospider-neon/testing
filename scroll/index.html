The Manufacturing
Process

Kevlar fibers are produced through solution polycondensation, followed by dry-wet spinning. The PPTA polymer solution in concentrated sulfuric acid is extruded through spinnerets, creating fibers with 70–75% crystallinity and extraordinary molecular alignment.
